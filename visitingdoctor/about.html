About VisitingDoctor

Bringing quality healthcare directly to your doorstep since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, VisitingDoctor was born from a simple yet powerful idea: everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, regardless of their ability to visit a clinic or hospital.

What started as a small team of dedicated doctors in London has grown into a nationwide network of healthcare professionals, all committed to providing exceptional medical care in the comfort of your home.

Today, we're proud to have served over 100,000 patients across the UK, maintaining our commitment to personalized care and professional excellence.

Our Mission

To make quality healthcare accessible to everyone by bringing professional medical services directly to patients' homes, ensuring comfort, convenience, and excellence in care delivery.

Our Vision

To revolutionize healthcare delivery by creating a world where quality medical care is available to everyone, anywhere, at any time.
